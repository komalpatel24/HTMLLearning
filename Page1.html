PTag

Paragraph Tag


  • The P tag offers a way to structure your text into different paragraphs.
  • Each paragraph of text should go in between an opening P and a closing /p tag.
  • In this article, we will know the HTML Paragraphs, & its basic implementation through the examples. The p tag in HTML defines a paragraph.
  • Most browsers read a line as a paragraph even if we don't use the closing tag i.e, /p , but this may raise unexpected results.
  • So anything mentioned within ptag and /p is treated as a paragraph.
  • So, it is both a good convention, and we must use the closing tag.
  • These have both opening and closing tags.

Syntax: 
     
         ( (p)  Content  (/p)  )
